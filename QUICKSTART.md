# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - Dr.C StarSound

Get up and running in **2 minutes**!

## 1️⃣ Launch the App

**Option A: Direct Open**

- Double-click `index.html`
- Opens in your default browser

**Option B: Online**

- Visit: https://starsound.netlify.app

## 2️⃣ Start Audio (Required!)

1. Click the big **"▶ Start Audio Engine"** button
2. Wait for "Audio running" message
3. You're ready to make sound!

## 3️⃣ Make Your First Sound

### **Mouse Mode (Easiest)**

1. Click **"Mouse/Touch [M]"** button (should be already selected)
2. Move your cursor over the stars on the right side
3. Hear them sing! ✨

### **Auto Scan Mode (Automated)**

1. Click **"Auto Scan [S]"** button
2. Click **"▶ Start Scan"**
3. Watch the blue line sweep across the sky
4. Stars light up and make sound as they're scanned
5. Click **"⏸ Stop Scan"** to stop

### **Region Select Mode (Focused)**

1. Click **"Region Select [R]"** button
2. Click and drag a rectangle around stars
3. Click **"▶ Start Scan"**
4. Only stars in your rectangle will be scanned

## 4️⃣ Try a Preset

### **Mono Presets** (Single Sound)

1. Find **"Select Preset"** dropdown (top of left panel)
2. Choose any preset, like:
   - **"Melodic Journey"** - Musical bells
   - **"Cosmic Cathedral"** - Ethereal vocals
   - **"Glass Marimba Orchestra"** - Bright percussion
3. Preset loads instantly!

### **Multitimbral Presets** (4 Layered Sounds)

1. Change **"Voice Mapping"** to **"Multitimbral"**
2. New controls appear!
3. Select from **"Multitimbral Presets"** dropdown:
   - **"🌌 Cosmic Choir"** - Vocal ensemble
   - **"🔔 Bell Garden"** - Layered bells
   - **"👽 Alien Forest"** - Exotic organic sounds
4. Stars at different heights trigger different instruments!

## 5️⃣ Explore Sonification Modes

### **Mode 1: Position/Brightness [Press 1]**

- Left stars = Low pitch, Right stars = High pitch
- Bright stars = Loud, Dim stars = Quiet
- **Default mode**

### **Mode 2: Color/Temperature [Press 2]**

- Hot blue stars = High pitch
- Cool red stars = Low pitch
- Automatically switches to temperature-based synthesis

### **Mode 3: Constellation Patterns [Press 3]**

- Stars trigger percussion based on constellation
- Each zodiac constellation has unique rhythms
- Great for exploring patterns

## 6️⃣ Randomize Everything!

**Press `X` key** or click **"🎲 RANDOMIZE ALL SETTINGS"**

- Instantly shuffles ALL settings
- Discovers wild new combinations
- Try it 10 times in a row for sonic exploration!

| ------- | --------------------------- |
| `M`     | Mouse/Touch mode            |

## 🎛️ Key Controls to Adjust

### **Pitch Range**

- Slider: **100-3000 Hz** (default)
- Try: **50-6000 Hz** for extreme range
- Try: **200-800 Hz** for musical range

### **Reverb Mix**

- Slider: **0-100%**
- **0%** = Dry, direct sound
- **80%+** = Huge cosmic space

### **Scan Speed** (Auto Scan mode)

- Slider: **1-20 seconds**
- **2s** = Fast, rhythmic
- **15s** = Slow, meditative

### **Scan Radius** (Mouse mode)

- Slider: **30-250 pixels**
- **Small** = Trigger one star at a time
- **Large** = Trigger many stars (chords)

## 🔊 No Sound?

### **Checklist**

1. ✅ Did you click **"▶ Start Audio Engine"**?
2. ✅ Is your system volume up?
3. ✅ Try refreshing the page (F5)
4. ✅ Check browser console (F12) for errors
5. ✅ Try Chrome/Edge (best compatibility)

### **Still No Sound?**

- **Check Voice Limit**: Too many simultaneous notes?
- **Reduce Scan Radius**: Try 80px
- **Reduce Reverb**: Try 30%
- **Check Solo/Mute**: In multitimbral mode, voices may be muted
